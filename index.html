Before You Visit the Clinic

Carrying a few essentials helps us start your treatment faster and build an accurate, personalized rehab plan.

Bring Reports

Bring previous X-ray, MRI, surgery notes, prescriptions, and blood reports (if relevant).

Wear Comfortable Clothing

Choose loose, movement-friendly clothes so assessment and exercises can be done comfortably.

Share Full History

  • When pain started and what worsens or relieves it.
  • Any previous injury, surgery, or ongoing medicine.
